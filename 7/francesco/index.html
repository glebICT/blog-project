• What Is an FRS or BRZ Drift Car?
  • FRS stand for Front-engine, Rear-wheel drive and Sport, a car that has all three of these is an FRS car. usually most drift cars are FRS but some are also BRZ. BRZ stands for Boxer, Rear-wheel drive and Zenith, incase you are wondering Boxer and Zenith have their own meanings, The "Boxer" engine is in every Subaru car and Zenith stands for "As Fast As Possible".
