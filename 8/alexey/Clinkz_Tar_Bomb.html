Tar Bomb Clinkz throws a projectile filled with tar at the target enemy, dealing Magic damage on impact. After impact, the tar spills onto the ground and stays there for 5 seconds. Any enemy that walks across it gets slowed, and attacks from Clinkz or his Burning Army Skeleton Archers will deal bonus physical damage. When the Tar Bomb hits, any Burning Army skeletons within attack range will attack the target.
