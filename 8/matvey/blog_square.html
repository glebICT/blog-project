Square

A square is a regular quadrilateral, which means that it has four sides of equal length and four 90-degree angles (π2 radian angles). It can also be defined as a rectangle with two equal-length adjacent sides.

It is the only regular polygon whose internal angle, central angle, and external angle are all equal (90°), and whose diagonals are all equal in length.
