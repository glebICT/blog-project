Qubit basis states

Single-qubit computational basis states
1 2 3

ψ=α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩ A general -pure- qubit state is expressed as: ψ=α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩ where α and β are the complex probability amplitudes for each basis state. Note that the choice of basis states is arbitrary, each set of orthogonal states can be used as basis states.
