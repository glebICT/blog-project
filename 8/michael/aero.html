Windows Aero

Windows Aero (Authentic, Energetic, Reflective, and Open) was the design style of Windows that was the mainstream style for operational systems of Microsoft from the creation of Vista as we know it (after Longhorn) until the end of Windows 8's first beta release.

Aero became popular within the popular culture, especially with the popularisation of Frutiger Aero aesthetic, although nowadays it's not as popular, you cannot deny that it left a massive footstep in the personal computer's history, as Windows 7 is sometimes considered as peak work of Microsoft.
