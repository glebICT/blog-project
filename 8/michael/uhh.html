Who am I?

I am a user of several social media sites like Reddit (usually hide my name behind a pseudoname "sprait"), mod enthusiast in Steam community, a minor game developer on Roblox. Although I do not have any loud projects, I would still like to address some of interesting things I have done in the internet:

1. A soviet-styled multiplayer game on Roblox made for a Discord community, features FPS (first-person shooter) and RP (roleplay) gameplay. Currently in development.

2. A map for a game on Steam called "Workers & Resources", that brought some attention.

3. A discontinued YouTube gameplay series of the previously mentioned "Workers & Resources" game, the biggest watch count I've ever had was on one of these videos: it took 1,3k views. This may not seem as a big number among millions of views on some YouTube videos, but considering the fact that "Workers & Resources" has a community of around 30-50k people, such views are not just big, they collect a big fraction of my favourite game's community.

Unfortunately, I cannot continue doing things I used to do, such as previously mentioned videos, as my desktop is left in Russia, and my current setup can't keep up with recording software running parallel to the game, and this really upsets me. But I hope everything will one day be as usual, allowing usual people like me to live their usual lives, without any hiccups or bad surprises.

Sadly, the financial situation for us gets worse each day. I usually don't like saying this (only among close friends), but the situation of mine is upsetting. Since I study in a private school (believe me or not, there weren't other variants of education for me), I study among rich immigrants (usually from Moscow), which makes for a "not the best" condition for, say, socialisation. I'm glad that I have friends who care about friendship more than about money, allowing me to socialise in this unfair world. I'm not sure whether I would be able to continue studying in this school.
