PATRICK STAR

A funny spongebob character

Patrick Star is one of the ten main characters of the animated series SpongeBob SquarePants,He is SpongeBob's best friend as well as one of his two neighbors.He lives under a rock in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob and Squidward are his two neighbors, and, when viewed on the front side, are to the right of his residence.

Sheldon J. Plankton, most commonly known as Plankton, is one of the ten main characters of the SpongeBob SquarePants franchise.[5] He is a planktonic copepod who runs the Chum Bucket restaurant alongside Karen, a waterproof computer who is his sidekick and wife. The Chum Bucket is unpopular in Bikini Bottom because it serves chum, a type of bait made from fish meat.

Gary The Snail

Garold Gareth "Gary" Wilson, Jr. (also frequently called "Gare" by SpongeBob) is SpongeBob SquarePants' pet sea snail. He lives with SpongeBob in his pineapple house on 124 Conch Street.He is a domesticated house pet with similar mannerisms to the real-world cat, most notably his "meow" sound, as snails are the underwater equivalent to cats in the show.
