06 NAGOYA

This city holds many interesting things. Here are some of them.

Nagoya City Science Museum

The museum houses one of the largest planetariums in the world and has three main sections on modern technology, life sciences and general science with a variety of hands-on exhibits.
The upper floor has tablets, and many informative parts about space and future technology.

Tokugawa Art Museum

In the house there are over 10 000 artifacts. There you can find swords, katanas, and many other interesting stuff.
There you will learn about samurai culture, what they wore and how they fought.
This part of the culture seems very interesting.
