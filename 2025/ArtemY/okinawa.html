04 OKINAWA

Famous by its beach and relaxed atmosphere.
Maybe you know, maybe you don't, but most of the Japanese are disciplined and hard-working. In Okinawa, people are very relaxed! The water is clear, the white buildings are in great harmony with the sea.
Here are some fancy places in Okinawa City:

Busena Marine Park

In this park you can see different types of fishes, like in an aquarium.
But here, fish will swim naturally. They're not stuck in a place called a human-made aquarium, they live there. They are not surrounded by walls.
This is a great experience.

Okinawa Japan Temple

Okinawa Japan Temple is a Christian temple. It looks very unusual, like it's not a temple at all.
The temple combines Japnese, American, and Christian style, with something unique added.
